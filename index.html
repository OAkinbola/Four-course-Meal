Four-course Meal

A Four-course Meal is a meal that contains four distinct courses. it consists of the Appetizer Course, Soup, an Entrée, and a Dessert.

According to the Carnegie Museums, Formal four-course Meal are served either in Russian Style or French style.when the meal served in Russian style, each course is brought to the table at the ptoper time but if served in the french style, all of the courses are produced simultaneously and stay on the table for the duration of the meal.

Frozen desserts, however, stay chilled until the dessert course.

APPETIZERS

An appetizer which is also know as an hors d'eouvre is a small dish served before a meal to stimulate your appetite.They are intended to be eaten by hand in a bite or two, or at least be capable of being cut with the side of a fork, rather than a knife.
